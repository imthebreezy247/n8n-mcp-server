## Copyright Law Overview

## Fair Use Doctrine (US)

Fair use MAY allow limited use of copyrighted material for:
- Commentary and criticism
- News reporting
- Teaching and education
- Parody and transformative works

### Four Fair Use Factors:

1. **Purpose and character** of use
   - Commercial vs. educational
   - Transformative nature

2. **Nature of copyrighted work**
   - Published vs. unpublished
   - Creative vs. factual

3. **Amount used**
   - 2 minutes from a 2-hour movie = ~1.7%
   - BUT: Quality matters more than quantity
   - Using the "heart" of the work is problematic

4. **Effect on market value**
   - Does it harm original's market?
   - Could it substitute for the original?

### Fair Use Analysis for This Project

**Likely NOT Fair Use because:**

- **Purpose**: Entertainment on TikTok (not educational/critical)
- **Commercial**: TikTok monetization opportunities
- **Amount**: Taking the "best" 2 minutes
- **Market Effect**: Could reduce desire to watch original
- **Not transformative**: Simply re-posting existing content

**Consult a lawyer** for specific fair use questions.

## Required Legal Steps Before Production Use

### 1. Consult Legal Counsel

**Before launching, consult with:**
- Entertainment lawyer
- Intellectual property attorney
- Media law specialist

**Questions to ask:**
- Do my use cases qualify for fair use?
- What licenses do I need?
- What are my liability risks?
- How should I structure my business?

### 2. Obtain Necessary Licenses

**Contact rights holders:**
- Movie studios (Warner Bros, Universal, Disney, etc.)
- TV networks
- Distribution companies
- Music rights holders (if using audio)

**Get written agreements covering:**
- Scope of use
- Duration of license
- Territorial restrictions
- Attribution requirements
- Modification permissions

### 3. Implement Content ID Checking

**Before posting, verify:**
- TikTok Content ID won't flag it
- YouTube Content ID (test upload)
- Check against known copyrighted databases

**Tools:**
- Google Video Intelligence API
- YouTube Copyright Match Tool
- TikTok's Copyright Library

### 4. Set Up Legal Protection

**Business structure:**
- Form LLC or corporation
- Separate personal and business liability
- Get business insurance
- E&O (Errors & Omissions) insurance

**Documentation:**
- Keep records of all licenses
- Document fair use analysis
- Maintain usage logs
- Save all permissions

### 5. Create Attribution System

**Give proper credit:**
- Movie title and year
- Studio/distributor
- Copyright notice
- License information (if applicable)

**Example:**

## DMCA Compliance

### If You Receive a DMCA Takedown:

1. **Do not ignore it**
   - Legal consequences for non-compliance
   - Platform will remove content

2. **Evaluate the claim**
   - Is it valid?
   - Do you have rights to the content?
   - Does fair use apply?

3. **Options:**
   - Accept and remove content
   - File counter-notice (if you believe it's fair use)
   - Seek legal counsel

4. **Learn from it**
   - Avoid similar content in future
   - Improve content screening

### DMCA Counter-Notice

Only file if you truly believe you have the right to use the content:

- Must include specific information
- Can result in legal action from claimant
- Consult lawyer before filing
